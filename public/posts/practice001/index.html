Macro#

  • q{character} to start recording a macro in register {character}
  • q to stop recording
  • @{character} replays the macro
  • Macro execution stops on error
  • {number}@{character} executes a macro {number} times
  • Macros can be recursive first clear the macro with q{character}q record the macro, with @{character} to invoke the macro recursively (will be a no-op until recording is complete)
